**English** is a [West Germanic language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Germanic_language) that emerged in [early medieval England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Early_medieval_England) and has since become a global [lingua franca](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lingua_franca).([4])([5])([6]) The namesake of the language is the [Angles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angles_(tribe)), one of the [Germanic peoples](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germanic_peoples) who [migrated to Britain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anglo-Saxon_settlement_of_Britain) after the [end of Roman rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/End_of_Roman_rule_in_Britain). English is the [most spoken language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Most_spoken_language) in the world, primarily due to the global influences of the former [British Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Empire) (succeeded by the [Commonwealth of Nations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commonwealth_of_Nations)) and the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). It is the most widely learned [second language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_language) in the world, with more second-language speakers than native speakers. However, English is only the [third-most spoken native language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_languages_by_number_of_native_speakers), after [Mandarin Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandarin_Chinese) and [Spanish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_language).([3])

**英语**（英语：English）是目前全球使用最广、最具影响力的[通用语](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E9%80%9A%E7%94%A8%E8%AA%9E)，属[西日耳曼语支](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%A5%BF%E6%97%A5%E8%80%B3%E6%9B%BC%E8%AA%9E%E6%94%AF)，源自[中世纪早期的英格兰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E7%9B%8E%E6%A0%BC%E9%B2%81-%E6%92%92%E5%85%8B%E9%80%8A%E8%8B%B1%E6%A0%BC%E5%85%B0)。英语是世界上[母语人口第三多的语言](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E4%BB%A5%E6%AF%8D%E8%AA%9E%E4%BA%BA%E5%8F%A3%E6%8E%92%E5%BA%8F%E7%9A%84%E8%AA%9E%E8%A8%80%E5%88%97%E8%A1%A8)（仅次于[汉语](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E8%AA%9E)和[西班牙语](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E8%AA%9E)([4])），有[近六十个国家](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%8B%B1%E8%AA%9E%E5%9C%8B%E5%AE%B6%E5%92%8C%E5%9C%B0%E5%8D%80%E5%88%97%E8%A1%A8)将其作为唯一或主要的[官方语言](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%AE%98%E6%96%B9%E8%AA%9E%E8%A8%80)，居世界之冠。英语作为当今的[世界语言](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E8%AA%9E%E8%A8%80)([5])([6])，是[联合国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%81%AF%E5%90%88%E5%9C%8B)、[欧盟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%AD%90%E7%9B%9F)、[国际民航组织](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E9%9A%9B%E6%B0%91%E8%88%AA%E7%B5%84%E7%B9%94)等众多[国际组织](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E9%9A%9B%E7%B5%84%E7%B9%94)的官方语言、[互联网](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E4%BA%92%E8%81%94%E7%BD%91)的法通语([7])，也是学习人数最多的[外语](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%A4%96%E8%AA%9E)。
